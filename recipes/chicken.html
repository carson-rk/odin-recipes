Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved Indian-inspired dish featuring marinated chicken pieces cooked in a creamy, spiced tomato sauce. The bold spices combined with the smooth sauce make it a perfect comfort meal to enjoy with rice or naan bread. Its rich flavor profile appeals to both spice lovers and those new to Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • teaspoon turmeric
  • teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream or coconut milk
  • Salt
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, half the garlic and ginger, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, chili powder, and salt. Add chicken pieces and coat well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
  2. Cook the chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. Make the sauce: In the same pan, heat remaining oil. Add onions, remaining garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions are soft and golden.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices: Stir in crushed tomatoes, remaining garam masala, and salt. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  5. Finish the sauce: Lower heat and stir in cream or coconut milk. Return chicken to the pan and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with basmati rice or naan.
